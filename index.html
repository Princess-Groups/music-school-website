Our Story

Music Excellence in Nagercoil

Located in the heart of Nagercoil, Shone Music School has been a trusted name in music education since its establishment in 2000. With over 26 years of experience, we have built a strong reputation for nurturing talent and delivering high-quality musical training to students of all ages.

We are proudly associated with the internationally renowned Trinity College London, providing students with globally recognized certifications and a structured learning approach that meets international standards.

At Shone Music School, we believe music is more than just learning an instrument—it's about expressing creativity, building confidence, and developing discipline. Our team of experienced instructors offers personalized guidance in both instrumental and vocal training.
